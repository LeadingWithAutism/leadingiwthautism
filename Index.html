Trevor J. Semeniuk, CD, BSc, MASc is an experienced senior leader in the Canadian Armed Forces who embraces transformational, people-centred leadership, and wise compassion management. Skilled in Command, Leadership, and Senior-Level human and material resources management. Proficient in the procurement of technical services and management of IT Professionals. He supports inclusive practices and believes strongly in diversity, being a champion of neuroinclusion. He possesses a BSc and MASc in Electrical Engineering and is working towards an Executive MBA. His current research focus is on the intersection of autism and leadership.

There is an increasing business case for hiring Autistic Leaders due to traits like pattern recognition, detail orientation, unique perspectives, and increased organizational loyalty, however, there are also demonstrated difficulties that Autistic individuals experience in the workplace, such as communication misunderstandings and social anxiety. With reasonable accommodations, we can remove systematic barriers, eliminate stigma, provide support, and ensure psychological safety to the under-represented autistic community.
